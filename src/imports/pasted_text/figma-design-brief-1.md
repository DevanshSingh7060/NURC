Continuation & Refinement of Figma Design Project Brief: Clean Reader-First Identity + Responsive Enhancements
Design Refinements – Creating Our Own Premium Identity
Shift from the previous references to a super clean, minimalist, and highly reader-friendly aesthetic that establishes NURC’s unique identity as a trusted, no-nonsense B2B intelligence provider.
New Design Direction (Inspired by 2026 B2B Trends):

Extremely clean and spacious (inspired by minimalist corporate sites like McKinsey, Berkshire Hathaway’s simplicity, and clean B2B examples from Webflow/HubSpot).
Prioritize readability and calm professionalism over visual flair — perfect for a reader-focused newsletter platform.
Unique NURC Identity: Subtle elegance with precise typography, generous whitespace, soft shadows, and a refined corporate palette that feels premium yet approachable. Avoid anything trendy or distracting.

Updated Typography System (More Reader-Friendly)

Headings: Use Satoshi or Geist Sans (modern, clean geometric sans-serif with excellent legibility).
Body Text: Inter or Source Sans Pro (highly readable on screens, optimized for long-form reading with 1.8+ line height).
Base sizes: 17–18px body on desktop, 16px on mobile. Generous line spacing and paragraph breaks for reduced eye strain.
This creates a calm, editorial feel that sets us apart while staying professional.

Color Palette Refinement (Light Theme Only – Premium & Calm)

Primary Navy: #0A2540 (deep, trustworthy)

Accent: Warm Neutral Gold #C8B39E (very subtle)

Surface/Cards: Pure #FFFFFF with soft 4–8px shadow
Text: #1F2937 (primary), #374151 (secondary)


Client Showcase – Attractive & Prominent (Using Real Logos)
Make the client relationships a major credibility driver. When a visitor from another company opens the site, they should immediately feel the premium network NURC has.

Main Client Bar (New Feature): Implement a subtle, constantly moving horizontal marquee right after the Hero section (and optionally in Footer).
Use actual high-quality official logos (Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Volvo, Tata Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, Bosch, Maruti, Hyundai, Honda, Crisil, HDFC Life, Deutsche Bank, etc.).
Direction: Smooth left-to-right continuous scroll (very slow speed — 30–45 seconds per loop).
Style: Clean white background, logos in grayscale or original colors with consistent height (40–55px), generous spacing, subtle hover pause + slight gold tint.
Performance: CSS-only or lightweight implementation for fast loading. Pause on hover for accessibility.
Heading above bar: “Trusted by India’s Leading Industry Leaders” (clean, understated).

Additional placements: Prominent static grid on Homepage (below marquee) and dedicated Clients page with industry filters. This dual approach (moving + static) attracts attention without being overwhelming.

Subtle Animations (Performance-First – B2B Premium)

Keep the site light and fast-loading (target Core Web Vitals 90+).
Allowed subtle animations only:
Client marquee horizontal scroll (as above).
Card hover: Gentle 4–6px lift + soft shadow increase (200ms ease).
Button hover: Subtle scale (1.02x) + color shift.
Fade-in on scroll for sections (very light, 300ms).
Reader Mode open: Smooth overlay transition.

No parallax, heavy scroll animations, or complex effects — prioritize speed for corporate users on various networks/devices.


Reader-First Experience Refinements

Even more whitespace and breathing room on all pages.
Consistent high readability: Larger body text, excellent contrast, and Reader Mode as a core feature.
Mobile: Extra padding, larger tap targets, simplified stacked layouts.
